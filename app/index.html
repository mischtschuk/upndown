Sip hot chocolate with marshmallows You can`t beat a steaming cup of hot chocolate on a cold winter day. Here is a recipe of your cozy evening: Ingredients: 
1 cup milk 
1/4 cup half and half
 1/4 cup milk chocolate chips 
1 tablespoon creamy peanut butter 
1/4 cup mini marshmallows  Method:
Bring milk and cream to a simmer in a small saucepan over medium-low heat (do not boil). When the milk begins to steam, add the chocolate. Stir until the chocolate melts. Add the peanut butter and stir until just melted. Pour into a large mug. Top with mini marshmallows.

Drink eggnog Is Eggnog a part of your family traditions? There is no better way to get into the Christmas spirit than with a glass of eggnog. Here is a recipe: Ingredients:
1 quart eggnog 
3/4 c. white chocolate chips
 1/3 c. crushed candy canes  
whipped cream 
coarsely chopped candy canes (for sprinkling on top) Method:
Combine eggnog, white chocolate chips, and crushed candy canes in a saucepan over low heat. Heat, stirring constantly, until white chocolate has melted and the mixture is heated through. Serve warm, topped with whipped cream and sprinkled with coarsely chopped candy cane pieces.
